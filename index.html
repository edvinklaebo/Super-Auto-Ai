PoE Mechanics

Each hit applies at most one poison stack. Poison DPS = hit damage × scaling × (1 + increased DoT) × DoT multiplier. Duration = base duration × (1 + increased duration). Poison stacks are unlimited. Only Chaos resistance mitigates poison damage. Total Poison DPS is hard-capped at ~35.8 M/s (32-bit integer limit ÷ 60 Hz tick rate).
